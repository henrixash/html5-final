CBE Dean joins learning visit in Belgium

By Abigail Faith C. Chinaman || Date Posted: September 14, 2023

Participated by six delegates from the Philippines, a Learning Visit is organized on June 21-30, 2023 in collaboration with West Flanders Synergy Pilipinas and the Province of West Flanders, Belgium.

NVSU focal person for Synergy Pilipinas and Dean of the College of Business Education, Dr. Dolores B. Galvez joined the said program which gave her the opportunities to explore and gain insights into various technological advancements and practices in several institutions.

Dr. Galvez visited two competence-based international universities including the VIVES University of Applied Sciences known for its innovative to practical research and its services provided to society, and the Howest University of Applied Sciences renowned for its technology programs such as Cybersecurity and Digital Arts and Entertainment. They also dropped by the Food Innovation Park, Organic Farms, Provincial Technology Institute, and Inagro.

With the intention to observe and adapt from the latest technologies in Belgium to the Philippines, Dr. Galvez plans to support our young entrepreneurs in starting their business endeavors and develop the existing innovative centers like the NVSU Sabatan Technopreneurs Hub.

CBE submits BSBA for AACCUP LEVEL III ACCREDITATION

By IPPRC Coordinator || Date Posted: May 31, 2023

To achieve a culture of quality and excellence, the CBE?s Bachelor of Science in Business Administration major in Financial Management and Marketing Management was re-visited and accredited online by the AACCUP for LEVEL III Phase 2 on May 29-31, 2023.

The CBE Team prepared and uploaded research, extension, faculty, and linkages/consortia documents. Dr. Ivy Mar Ramos and Dr. Sisendo Masangca Jr. thoroughly evaluated these documents and despite the premeditated power outage, the accreditation task force was able to defend and respond to the accreditors' questions during the Q&A segment.

CBE CELEBRATES 13th FOUNDING ANNIVERSARY

By IPPRC Coordinator || Date Posted: May 26, 2023

The College of Business Education celebrated its 13th Founding Anniversary on May 25-26, 2023 with the theme "13th Year of CBE's Feat in Building Resilience and Achieving Excellence" conducted at University Gymnasium. Throughout the two-day event, numerous activities were carried out. On the first day, Rev. Msgr. Quezon A. Juan officiated over a Thanksgiving Mass, which was followed by the Opening Program, where Dr. Michael M. Quilang, CEO of Top Achievers Private Schools, was the featured speaker.

On the second day, the college recognized the invaluable services, outstanding achievements, and leadership of the former Deans, as well as the students' exemplary academic performance, leadership, and cultural and athletic competition. Ms. Marilou B. Duque, a CBE benefactor, Prof. Ruby C. Sansano, and Dr. Cristina R. Salvosa, former deans of the CBE, delivered their heartwarming Inspirational Messages during the Recognition Program.

Students exhibited their innovative products at the CBE Tienda, participated in a team-building exercise, and displayed their aptitude and creativity on the CBE Runway.

Graduate students host the third Management Conference at the NVSU Mini Theatre on June 3, 2023. The centerpieces of this event are the GS Graduating Student paper presentations and the Seminar-Workshop on Research Writing, for which Dr. Caroline M. Acosta serves as the lecturer.

NVSU student shines in NATCCO Youth Congress 2023

By Angel L. Genato IV || Date Posted May 8, 2023

The Nueva Vizcaya State University partook in the National Confederation of Cooperatives (NATCCO) Youth Congress at the Sugarland Hotel in Bacolod City from May 2 to 4, 2023. The NVSU student delegation of nine (9) student leaders from the Supreme Student Council-Bayombong Campus (SSC-Bay), College of Business Education Student Council (CBE-SC), Junior Financial Executives (JFINEX)-NVSU, Philippine Junior Marketing Association (PJMA)-NVSU were among the 108 participants, including 34 youth-led cooperatives.

During the Congress, Mr. Jean Chloe Miray, a Bachelor of Science in Business Administration, majoring in Marketing third-year student, won the Quiz bee Coop-youth Masterminds Ace Category.

The Congress also served as an opportunity for students to share their knowledge and ideas about cooperatives with fellow participants.

The NVSU student delegation is expected to bring back valuable insights to the University towards the development of economic enterprise development by as mandated by the Commission on Higher Education.

Ms. Katrine Angela Tucay, Economic Enterprise Development Unit Head of the Student Affairs & Services-Bayombong Campus and SSC-Bay Adviser, and Ms. Cherrie Prelle de Guzman, CBE-SC Adviser, chaperoned the NVSU student delegation to the Congress.
